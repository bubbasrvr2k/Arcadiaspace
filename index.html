Our Mission

"Arcadia is a non-profit corporation dedicated to bring young Engineers and Scientists together to learn and experience real life applications of the subjects learned at the university. This is why we measure each member’s capacity to work as a team and individual hard-skills. On the white comet project we decided to find the best scientists that UTD is brewing.
It is expected from all members to be the most professional and proficient in their interests out of all other science related organizations on campus, but it is also imperative to pursue their studies with the best they can offer. This makes for a future scientist with vast knowledge and experience."
